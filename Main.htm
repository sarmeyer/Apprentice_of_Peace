Our Program

At the Apprentice of Peace Wellness Studio, our goal is to provide the best in total health, wellness, and fitness. Our classes are a combination of Yoga, Qi Gong, and Tai Chi and are designed to help with relaxation, improve and promote better breathing, increase range of motion and energy, and relieve tension. They are low impact routines which aid in healing and improving balance and flexibility. We also have certified massage therapists on staff to provide soothing and stimulating massages including Swedish, deep tissue, oncology, sports injury, acupressure, and much more. Let us work with you to design a health and wellness plan that fits your needs.
